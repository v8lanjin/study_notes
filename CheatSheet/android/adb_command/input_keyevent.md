### 命令：input keyevent

参考链接[keyevent.html](https://developer.android.com/reference/android/view/KeyEvent.html)  

常用按键键值

| Key             | Value         |
|:---------------:|:-------------:|
|**KEYCODE_POWER**| `26 (0x1a)`   |
|**KEYCODE_MENU** | `82`          |
|**KEYCODE_HOME** | `3`           |
